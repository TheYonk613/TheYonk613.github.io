My Journey

Welcome! I'm Yonk — part business mind, part creative soul.

My background is a bit unconventional, but that's what makes the work I do so adaptable. I've managed executive operations in the renewable energy space, consulted in the diamond industry, supported real estate development projects, and helped scale non-profits with a mission close to my heart. I've worked in media, finance, and marketing — often wearing multiple hats at once, and always learning as I go.

At my core, I love figuring things out quickly and turning complexity into clarity. Whether it's implementing a new CRM, managing a multi-step logistics process, writing content, or troubleshooting tech issues, I find satisfaction in getting things running smoothly behind the scenes.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me behind a drum kit — exploring new rhythms and refining my sound one beat at a time. Music is one of my ways of staying grounded and creative, always pushing to improve.

This site is a space to share what I've done, what I'm building, and what I'm still figuring out. Glad you're here.

Netanel Younker
